About Us


The sound world is our creation. The limit is your imagination.

EARTH ECLIPSED IS AN AWARD-WINNING FICTION PODCAST WITH A FULL CAST, IMMERSIVE SOUND DESIGN, AND A STORY TO KEEP YOU GLUED TO YOUR HEADPHONES. OUR TEAM WORKED REMOTELY WITH ACTORS ACROSS NINE STATES AND FOUR COUNTRIES TO DELIVER AN EXTRAORDINARY SCI-FI ADVENTURE WITH AN UNFORGETTABLE CAST OF CHARACTERS. EXPLORING CONTEMPORARY THEMES LIKE DATA PRIVACY AND THE BALANCE BETWEEN FREEDOM AND ORDER, EARTH ECLIPSED IS THE CULMINATION OF OUR CURIOSITY ABOUT THE SELF, OUR PHILOSOPHY OF MORALITY, AND OUR UNDERSTANDING OF THE HUMAN EXPERIENCE. IT’S AN EXCITING AND THOUGHT-PROVOKING RIDE THAT UNRAVELS A THRILLING MYSTERY AND LAUNCHES AUDIENCES INTO A UNIVERSE UNSEEN BEFORE.

HONORABLE MENTION

The Lunar Company was founded by three friends—Nick, AJ, and Victor—driven to tell exciting and immersive audio stories.

OUR TEAM MAY BE SMALL, BUT WE CHALLENGED OURSELVES TO DELIVER A MIGHTY, UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCE. WE HOPE YOU ENJOY EARTH ECLIPSED AND WE THANK YOU FOR IMAGINING THIS WORLD WITH US.

WE THOUGHT THE SKY WAS THE LIMIT UNTIL WE FOUND THE MOON.

NICK, AJ, AND VICTOR



WHY?
EARTH
ECLIPSED



AUDIO IS A MEDIUM THAT ALLOWS AUDIENCES TO IMAGINE AND DREAM AS THE SOUNDS PAINT A WORLD AROUND THEM. TO SHAPE THE WORLD AND PERSONALIZE THE CHARACTERS. THAT CLOSE CONNECTION FREES THE IMAGINATION AND MAKES PODCAST STORYTELLING UNIQUE. WE BELIEVE THAT OUR UNCOMPROMISING COMMITMENT TO STORY AND OUR DEDICATION TO MAINTAINING THE HIGHEST STANDARDS OF PRODUCTION WILL TRANSLATE INTO

OUTSTANDING STORIES THAT WILL LINGER IN OUR AUDIENCES’ MINDS LONG AFTER THE CREDITS ROLL. EARTH ECLIPSED IS ONLY OUR FIRST STEP INTO SOMETHING ‘LUNAR’—A TERM FROM THE UNIVERSE OF EARTH ECLIPSED THAT MEANS ‘SOMETHING THAT DOESN’T FIT NEATLY IN A BOX; SOMETHING FANTASTIC AND OTHER-WORLDLY.’ WE EMBRACE THAT DEFINITION AND BRING IT TO THE STORIES WE TELL, EXPLORING UNCHARTED PATHS INTO NEW MEDIA, AND DARING TO GO WHERE NO ONE HAS BEFORE.
